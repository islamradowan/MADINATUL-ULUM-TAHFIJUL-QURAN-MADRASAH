Thesis/Project Report:


1. Students of each group must have to submit their completed binding report before final defence.

2. Each group must submit their draft report to their supervisor for approval. Before binding the report it must be approved by the supervisor.

3. After successful completion of the final defence the report will be signed by the board of examiners on that day.

4. Few points about report writing:

Ø First Section: From top page to starting of first chapter the page number (footer) will be in roman.

Ø The number of the top page will be invisible and it will be shown from the 2nd page from roman ii.

Ø Second Section: From first chapter to the end the page number will be integer (From 1 to n).

Ø Contents of First Section:

Title (Top) Page

Declaration

Approval

Acknowledgement

Abstract

Table of Content (will be started from Abstract)

List of Figures

List of Tables

Ø Contents of Second Section:

Chapters:

1. Introduction

2. Literature Review

3. Problem Statement

4. Analysis and Design of Proposed System (Project)

Analysis of Existing Solution (Thesis)


5. Implementation and Testing (Project)

Analysis of Proposed Solution (Thesis)

6. Conclusion and Future Work


References (will not be treated as chapter and have to follow IEEE

references for writing)


Ø Writing Style:

Title: Times New Roman, Black, Bold

Title 1- Size 18

Title 2- Size 16

Title 3- Size 14

Description: Times New Roman, Black, Size 12, Justified


Ø Figures:

Figures copy from book or internet will not be allowed.

All figures must be drawn by students.
